# Smart Design Digital Pro - Quick Guide

## How to Use (Double-Click These Files)

| File | What It Does |
|------|-------------|
| **START WEBSITE.bat** | Starts the website on your computer (localhost) |
| **MY WEBSITE.bat** | Opens your live website in browser |
| **ADMIN DASHBOARD.bat** | Opens your admin dashboard |
| **MANAGE PRODUCTS.bat** | Opens Sanity Studio to add/edit products |

## Step by Step

### To see your website on your computer:
1. Double-click **START WEBSITE.bat**
2. Wait for it to start
3. Your browser will open automatically
4. Website is at: http://localhost:3000

### To see your live website online:
1. Double-click **MY WEBSITE.bat**
2. Your website opens in browser

### To add products:
1. Double-click **MANAGE PRODUCTS.bat**
2. Sign in to Sanity
3. Click Product → Create new → Fill form → Publish

### To see your admin dashboard:
1. Double-click **ADMIN DASHBOARD.bat**
2. All your links are in one place
